Our Story

From Waste to hope: The Story Behind Velora

Every harvest season, the same heartbreaking story repeats itself across farming communities in Nigeria.

Before dawn, farmers head into their with hope. they invest their savings in seeds, water, labour and transportation, believing that months of hard work will provide for their families. Yet, too often, they watch basket of perfectly fresh tomatoes before there ever reach a market.

Not because the Tomaoes are not good enough.

But because there is no reliable system to preserve them.

For countless famillies, a season of sacrifice and loss.

We could not ignore that reality

Velora was born from a a simple conviction: no farmer should lose the reward of honest labour, and no nutritious food should go to waste when millions of famillies need it everyday.

Our Mission is to turn abundance into opportunity. By working directly with local farmers, we transform fresh tomatoes into premium, 100% organic tomato paste that preserves the natural taste, colour, and richness of real tomatoes without unnecessary preservatives or compromise.

Every jar of Velora tells a story.

It is the story of a farmer whose harvest was saved instead of discarded. A family whose income was protected. A community taking another step toward prosperity through agriculture.

We are not just making tomato paste.

We are helping build a future where Nigerian agriculture creates wealth, where local production reduces dependence on imports, and where farmers are respected as the foundation of a stronger food system.

We dream of a day when every farmer harvests with confidence instead of uncertainty, when post-harvest losses become the exception rather than the norm, and when kitchens across Africa are filled with products made from the finest locally grown tomatoes.

That is the purpose that drives us every single day.

Because at Velora, saving a tomato is about far more than preventing waste. It is about preserving dignity, protecting livelihoods, creating opportunities, and investing in the future of our nation.

From waste to hope. From harvest to opportunity. From farm to every home.
