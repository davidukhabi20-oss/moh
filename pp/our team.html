Meet Our Team

The dedicated experts behind the gold standard of tomato richness.

OCHUWA ILEBODU

Chief Executive Officer

Ochuwa Ilebodu is an accomplished Economist, Entrepreneur and business strategist with a strong academic and practical background in enterprise development and leadership. She holds a bachelor's degree in Economics from Kastina State University.

She further advanced her education at Ulster University, Birmingham United Kingdom, earning a master's in International Business with Human Resource Management, graduating with distinction.

Ochuwa is the founder of Evergreen Thrift and marketplace, a platform dedicated to empowering small business owners by providing structured thrift contributions and strategic marketing support.

As the Pioneer Chief Executive Officer of Velora Agroprocessing Ltd, Ochuwa brings strategic vision, operational discipline, and global business insight to accelerate Velora's growth trajectory within Nigeria's agroprocessing ecosystem.

Her appoiontment marks significant step in velora's commitment to excellence, innovation, and long-term impact in advancing food security and agro-industrial development

MUHAMMAD UMAR

CHIEF VISIONARY OFFICER

Muhammad Umar is a tax professional, investor, and entrepreneur with a passion for building sustainable businesses that create long-term economic value. He holds a Bachelor's degree in Business Administration from Bayero University, Kano.

With over five years of experience in taxation and business advisory, Muhammad has developed expertise in corporate taxation, financial strategy, investment analysis, and business development.

He is the Managing Partner of MH Kapital Advisory Limited, an investment and advisory firm that provides patient capital and strategic support to high-potential businesses.

Muhammad also leads the growth of Velora Tomato Paste, dedicated to producing premium, 100% organic tomato paste while strengthening local agricultural value chains.

MARYAM IBRAHIM

CHIEF OPERATING OFFICER

Maryam Ibrahim is a results-driven business strategist and operations executive with a strong background in project management, enterprise development, and large-scale event coordination.

Currently serving as the COO of Velora Agroprocessing Ltd, she oversees operations, supply chain systems, distribution strategy, and organizational growth.

Prior to her appointment as COO, Maryam founded Glittertand Global, an enterprise known for delivering high-impact business events, training programs, and capacity-building initiatives.
